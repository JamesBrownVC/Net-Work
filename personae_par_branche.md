# Personae par branche métier (pour organigramme complet)

But : élargir le mapping Sillage à TOUTES les fonctions d'un compte, pas seulement Produit/Marketing.
Sillage n'a qu'UN persona actif par workspace → on utilise un persona élargi (union de toutes ces branches).
Ce fichier documente le détail par branche (réutilisable si tu veux cibler une seule branche à la fois).

## 1. Direction / Exécutif
- Chief Executive Officer, Directeur Général, Président, General Manager, Managing Director, Chief Operating Officer
- Séniorité : owner, founder, c_suite

## 2. Sales / Commercial
- Chief Revenue Officer, VP Sales, Directeur Commercial, Head of Sales, Account Executive, Business Development Manager
- Séniorité : c_suite, vp, head, director, manager, senior

## 3. Marketing / Communication
- Chief Marketing Officer, VP Marketing, Directeur Marketing, Head of Marketing, Growth Manager, Brand Manager, Head of Communication, Head of Product Marketing
- Séniorité : c_suite, vp, head, director, manager, senior

## 4. Produit
- Chief Product Officer, VP Product, Head of Product, Product Manager, Product Owner
- Séniorité : c_suite, vp, head, director, manager, senior

## 5. Tech / Engineering
- Chief Technology Officer, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering, Engineering Manager, Tech Lead, Software Engineer, Solutions Architect
- Séniorité : c_suite, vp, head, director, manager, senior

## 6. Data / IA
- Chief Data Officer, Head of Data, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Head of AI
- Séniorité : c_suite, head, manager, senior

## 7. Finance
- Chief Financial Officer, Directeur Financier, Head of Finance, Financial Controller
- Séniorité : c_suite, vp, head, director, manager

## 8. RH / People
- Chief People Officer, DRH, Head of People, Talent Acquisition Manager, HR Business Partner
- Séniorité : c_suite, head, director, manager

## 9. Ops / IT / SI
- Chief Information Officer, DSI, Head of Operations, Head of IT
- Séniorité : c_suite, vp, head, director, manager

## 10. Legal / Compliance
- General Counsel, Directeur Juridique, Compliance Officer, Data Protection Officer
- Séniorité : c_suite, head, director, manager

---
Persona Sillage actif (id 26) = union de toutes ces branches, location France.
Pour re-cibler une seule branche : upsert_persona avec les titres de la branche voulue.
